剑桥少儿英语一级

Cambridge Young Learners English Level 1

Starters级别是孩子们英语学习的第一步，通过听、说、读、写四个方面培养孩子的基础英语能力。

The Starters level is children's first step in English learning, developing basic English skills through listening, speaking, reading, and writing.

剑桥少儿英语二级

Cambridge Young Learners English Level 2

Movers级别在Starters基础上进一步提升英语能力，加强词汇量和语法知识，提高听说读写技能。

The Movers level builds upon Starters to further enhance English abilities, strengthen vocabulary and grammar knowledge, and improve listening, speaking, reading, and writing skills.

剑桥少儿英语三级

Cambridge Young Learners English Level 3

Flyers级别是剑桥少儿英语的最高级别，全面提高英语综合运用能力，为更高层次英语学习打下基础。

The Flyers level is the highest level of Cambridge Young Learners' English, comprehensively improving English proficiency and laying the foundation for higher-level English learning.
